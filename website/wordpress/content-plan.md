# SealBridge Supply Website Content Plan

## Core Pages

### Home

Positioning: Custom Gasket Sourcing & Project Coordination Partner.

Primary message: Help electrical enclosure and control cabinet manufacturers source custom rubber, silicone, and foam gaskets from reliable production partners.

### Products

- Electrical Enclosure Gaskets
- Control Cabinet Sealing Strips
- EPDM Foam Gaskets
- Silicone Gaskets
- Adhesive Backed Die Cut Gaskets
- Custom Rubber Gaskets According to Drawing

Each product page should include:

- What it is
- Common materials
- Process options
- Typical applications
- Key parameters for quotation
- Compliance support

### Applications

- Outdoor Electrical Enclosures
- Control Cabinets
- Junction Boxes
- EV Charger Cabinets
- Solar Inverter Enclosures
- LED Lighting Housings
- Telecom Cabinets
- HVAC Equipment

### Quality & Compliance

Recommended wording:

SealBridge Supply helps coordinate material documents and compliance information such as RoHS, REACH, UL94 material grade information, TDS, and SDS when available from production partners. Final compliance suitability should be confirmed based on the selected material, supplier document, and project application.
